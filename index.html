Read our story

I have been doing hair for 30+ years!

The owner, Bonnie J. Wilson, graduated from the University of Washington. Bonnie found a Job through a Temporary job agency working for a major department in downtown Seattle. Shortly after working in the sportswear department, in June, one of the cosmetic vendors put up a list offering to train ten employees in the art of make-up artistry to work their cosmetics counter during the upcoming holiday season. Bonnie was one of the ten chosen. After working as a Make-Up artist for two years, Bonnie decided to become a Cosmetologist and opened her first salon in 1994 on Capital Hill in Seattle. Our Braid Stylist, UMOYA McKinney, was trained in braiding by her mother, Iman Finley, who is a phenomenal braider and has trained UMOYA well in the art of braiding.

Welcome to Bonnie Jean Hair Life Salon, where we strive to create the look and feel that each client believes is perfect for them.
